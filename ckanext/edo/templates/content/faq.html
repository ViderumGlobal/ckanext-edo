    FAQ

    Q. What if there is data I want but cannot find it?"

    A. Ask us! On our homepage, use the data request link.

    Q. How can people submit applications and visualizations?

    A. Go to the Apps page, where you’ll find information on all applications and visualizations that people have submitted so far, a link for submitting your own application and facilities for joining in discussions.

    Q. How can people use data.edostate.gov.ng?

    A. The portal uses a search engine to offer several ways of finding the data you want. You can view all the datasets to see everything that is currently available, or search by keyword, category or topics.

    Q. Is there a place for developers to discuss ideas, applications and using of the data?

    A. Yes, the blog is for that purpose. We realize that you’ll want to ask us and each other questions and we’ll try and help as best we can. There is also a vibrant community of developers in the State who meet regularly to discuss ideas around the portal. They can be contacted via email info@gsquad.net or website www.gsquad.net.

    Q. Will more public data become available in the future?

    A. Yes, this is just the start. As the project matures, more data will flow on to the site.

    Q. What are the commercial use rights if people have commercial ideas?

    A. The Creative Commons Attribute License enables you to use all our data for commercial purposes. We are delighted that you see a commercial opportunity in using our data.

    Q. Under what license is the data available?

    A. By “Creative Commons Attribute” License.

    Q. Are there any “do’s and don'ts?”

    A. We have a simple {% link_for _('terms of use'), controller='ckanext.edo.plugin:EdoController', action='tou' %} relating to your use of the data in your application. It covers how to relate your application data to the data we provide, and simple common-sense usage of the data. {% endblock %}
